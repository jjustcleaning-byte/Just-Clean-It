Who We Are

More Than a
Cleaning Company

Jackie Scott began her cleaning career in 2003 and has spent over two decades building a reputation for reliability and quality service across the RTP area and surrounding cities in North Carolina. In 2025, she rebranded and relaunched as Just Clean It, LLC — a reflection of her continued commitment to professional, high-standard cleaning for commercial and residential properties.

We serve a wide range of accounts — from behavioral health facilities and corporate offices to apartment community common areas, manufacturing facilities, and beyond. No matter the space, we show up with professionalism, reliability, and care.

  • Professionalism in every engagement
  • Reliable, trained independent contractors
  • Tailored service plans for every client
  • Care and integrity at our core
