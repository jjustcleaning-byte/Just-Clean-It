Our Story

Just Clean It, LLC was founded in 2003 by Jackie Scott with a simple belief: that a clean space creates a better quality of life for everyone. After over two decades of service, we rebranded in 2025 with a renewed commitment to our communities, our contractors, and the spaces we care for across the Durham–Raleigh–Triangle area.

What We Do

We provide professional commercial cleaning services to apartment communities, commercial facilities, and healthcare and behavioral health settings across Durham, Raleigh, Morrisville, Sanford, Pittsboro, and surrounding cities. Our independent contractors are the heart of everything we do.

Non-Compete Agreement

This Non-Compete Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Just Clean It, LLC ("Company"), a North Carolina limited liability company, and the independent contractor identified in this packet ("Contractor").

1. Independent Contractor Relationship

Contractor acknowledges they are engaged as an independent contractor and not as an employee. Nothing herein creates an employer-employee relationship.

2. Non-Solicitation of Clients

During engagement with Just Clean It, LLC and for twelve (12) months following termination for any reason, Contractor agrees not to directly or indirectly solicit, contact, or accept cleaning service business from any client or account of Just Clean It, LLC that Contractor served or had contact with during engagement.

3. Non-Solicitation of Contractors

Contractor agrees not to recruit, solicit, or encourage any other contractor or team member of Just Clean It, LLC to cease their engagement or to work for any competing business for twelve (12) months following the end of engagement.

4. Geographic Scope

This Agreement applies within the service territory of Just Clean It, LLC, including Durham, Raleigh, Morrisville, Sanford, Pittsboro, and surrounding Triangle-area communities in North Carolina.

5. Reasonableness

Contractor acknowledges the scope, duration, and geographic limitations are reasonable and necessary to protect the legitimate business interests of Just Clean It, LLC.

6. Remedy for Breach

In the event of breach or threatened breach, the Company shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief and any other available legal remedies.

7. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of North Carolina.

By signing, you confirm you have read and agree to the Non-Compete Agreement above.

Confidentiality Agreement

This Confidentiality Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Just Clean It, LLC ("Company") and the independent contractor identified in this onboarding packet ("Contractor").

1. Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" includes all non-public information disclosed by the Company, including client names and locations, facility access codes, pricing, contracts, business strategies, operational procedures, staffing arrangements, financial information, and any other information that should reasonably be understood to be confidential.

2. Contractor's Obligations

Contractor agrees to: (a) hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence; (b) not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent; (c) use Confidential Information solely in connection with performing contracted services; and (d) take all reasonable precautions to prevent unauthorized disclosure.

3. Client Site Confidentiality

Contractor acknowledges that work performed at client facilities may involve access to sensitive areas or information. Contractor agrees to maintain strict confidentiality regarding any client facilities, operations, personnel, or information observed while performing services.

4. Photographs and Social Media

Contractor shall not photograph, record, or post to social media any client sites, interiors, personnel, or identifiable location information without express written permission from Just Clean It, LLC and the relevant client.

5. Return of Information

Upon termination, Contractor shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information in their possession, including copies, notes, or records derived therefrom.

6. Duration

Confidentiality obligations survive termination and remain in effect indefinitely for trade secrets and for three (3) years following termination for all other Confidential Information.

7. Governing Law

Governed by the laws of North Carolina. Disputes shall be resolved in the courts of Durham County, North Carolina.

By signing, you confirm you have read and agree to the Confidentiality Agreement above.
