About Us

Family-Owned.
Community-Driven.

Just Clean It, LLC was founded in 2003 by Jackie Scott and has grown into a trusted commercial and residential property cleaning company serving the Durham and RTP area of North Carolina.

Rebranded in 2025, we serve a wide range of accounts — from behavioral health facilities and offices to luxury apartment community common areas and vacant unit turnovers. We don't just clean spaces, we maintain the standard that properties and businesses depend on.

  • Professionalism in every engagement
  • Reliable, trained independent contractors
  • Tailored service plans for every client
  • Care and integrity at our core

Opportunities

We work exclusively with independent contractors (1099). If you're a self-motivated cleaning professional with experience in luxury apartment communities, commercial facilities, or behavioral health environments — we want to hear from you.

What to Expect

Long-term contract placements at commercial and residential accounts across the Durham/RTP area. Compensation is based on full scope completion.

Requirements

  • Valid government-issued ID
  • Background check required
  • Drug screening required
  • Reliable transportation
  • Professional attitude & reliability
  • Direct deposit (ACH) for payment

Pay Structure

Contractors are compensated on a flat monthly or per-visit rate. Pay schedules vary by contract (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly).
